How to make a peanut butter and jelly sandwich

  1. Prepare two slices of bread
  2. Retrieve peanut butter and jelly from the cabinet or refridgerator
  3. Get butter knife and begin to spread peanut butter on both slices of bread
  4. Next, spread jelly on only one slice of bread
  5. Last, close both pieces of bread together and enjoy!
